Wikipedia is one of the most used sources for information online, but where is all this knowledge coming from? What many people don’t know is that every article contains a discussion page where the community reaches consensus on the article’s content.

Through the heated discussion behind the article on toilet paper orientation, this project the social mechanisms behind Wikipedia’s information.

The arrows on the bottom of the screen allow you to navigate through the story but feel free to get off track and dive deeper into the discussion page.
